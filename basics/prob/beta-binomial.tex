Beta-Binomial ($\BetaBin$)

&

$N \in \mathbb N, \alpha, \beta > 0$

&

$x \in \{0, \dotsc, N\}$

&

\(\displaystyle
	p(x) = \binom{N}{x} \frac{B(x + \alpha, N - x + \beta)}{B(\alpha, \beta)}
\)

& 

$\frac{N\alpha}{\alpha + \beta}$

&

$\frac{N\alpha\beta(\alpha + \beta + N)}{(\alpha + \beta)^2(\alpha + \beta + 1)}$
